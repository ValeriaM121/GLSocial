# GLSocial
## Product Vision
For fans of Girls Love (GL) shows and movies who are looking for a community, GLSocial is a mobile application that will allow users to discover GL content, connect with other fans, and participate in discussions about the shows they love. Unlike platforms that mainly focus on rating each episode and has a main focus on a single region’s content, GLSocial will contain GL shows and movies from around the world and will focus on bringing the community together. Users can either create or join either show or episode specific discussions, share reviews, recommend new series, and connect with others who share similar interest. The main goal of GLSocial is to be a platform where GL fans can have a space to discuss about how they feel about the show they are watching when there is nowhere else.
